Built for an operating auto repair shop, the application centralizes appointment booking, invoicing and customer relations in a single back office. Tax calculation (GST/QST) is automated on every invoice, and appointment reminders go out without manual intervention through scheduled jobs. Customer data handling was designed to meet the requirements of Quebec's Law 25 on personal information protection.
