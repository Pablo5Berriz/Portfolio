Logigest is a SaaS platform for farming and livestock operations in Sub-Saharan Africa, with a built-in accounting module for tracking the operation's finances. Its technical architecture relies on a strict hierarchy of AI agents — a project-manager agent supervising specialized agents — to structure feature development and delivery.
